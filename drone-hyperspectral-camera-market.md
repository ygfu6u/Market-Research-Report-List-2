<p><strong>The Drone Hyperspectral Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pushbroom Imaging</li><li>Frame-Based Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drone hyperspectral camera market consists primarily of two imaging types: pushbroom imaging and frame-based imaging. Pushbroom imaging captures spectral data by scanning across a scene in a linear fashion, allowing for high-resolution, continuous data collection over large areas, making it suitable for applications like agriculture and environmental monitoring. In contrast, frame-based imaging captures images in a single frame, providing higher spatial resolution for detailed analysis, which is ideal for industrial inspections and resource management. Both methods enhance remote sensing capabilities.</p></p>
