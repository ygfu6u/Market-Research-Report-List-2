<p><p>The acousto-optic modulators market includes two primary types: fiber-coupled and free-space acousto-optic modulators. Fiber-coupled modulators are designed to integrate easily with optical fibers, offering efficient modulation of light signals in telecommunications and data transfer applications. In contrast, free-space modulators manipulate light in open environments, making them ideal for laboratory settings and applications requiring precise control of laser beams. Both types are essential for various applications, including laser scanning, optical communications, and signal processing.</p></p>
