<p><p>The patio umbrellas market features three primary types: wall-mounted umbrellas, center pole umbrellas, and offset pole umbrellas. Wall-mounted umbrellas are attached to a wall and provide shade in compact areas, making them ideal for small patios. Center pole umbrellas have a central support that allows for uniform shade, often used in traditional outdoor settings. Offset pole umbrellas feature a cantilevered design that offers flexible shading options, perfect for larger spaces and outdoor dining areas, enhancing comfort and aesthetics.</p></p>
