<p><p>The solar thermal panels market includes three main types: flat-plate, tubular, and others. Flat-plate collectors are commonly used for residential heating applications due to their simple design and efficiency in converting sunlight into heat. Tubular collectors, often evacuated tubes, offer improved performance in colder climates as they minimize heat loss. The "others" category encompasses innovative designs and technologies, such as concentrating solar collectors, which are suited for large-scale power generation and industrial applications, enhancing overall market diversity and functionality.</p></p>
