<p><p>The solid-state overload relays market is primarily categorized into thermal and magnetic overload relays. Thermal overload relays operate based on heat generated by current flow, offering sensitivity to overload conditions and precise protection for motors. In contrast, magnetic overload relays utilize electromagnetic principles to detect overloads, providing rapid response and robust protection. Both types contribute to enhancing motor reliability and efficiency, with thermal relays being ideal for delicate applications and magnetic relays suited for high-power environments.</p></p>
