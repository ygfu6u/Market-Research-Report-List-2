<p><strong>The Oil Free Water Lubricated Air Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Helix</li><li>Double Helix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-free water-lubricated air compressor market includes two primary types: single helix and double helix compressors. Single helix compressors utilize a single helical rotor, allowing for efficient air compression with minimal maintenance due to the absence of oil. In contrast, double helix compressors feature two interlocking helical rotors, providing greater compression efficiency and improved airflow capabilities. Both types leverage water as a lubricant, promoting environmentally friendly operations while reducing the risk of contamination in applications requiring clean air.</p></p>
