<p><p>Single-feed check scanners are categorized into two main types based on their ultraviolet (UV) capabilities. UV-capable models are designed to detect and verify the authenticity of checks by scanning for specific UV features in the document. These models are essential for businesses that prioritize security against fraud. Conversely, models without UV capabilities focus solely on image capture and processing without the added layer of ultraviolet detection, making them a more cost-effective solution for basic check scanning needs.</p></p>
