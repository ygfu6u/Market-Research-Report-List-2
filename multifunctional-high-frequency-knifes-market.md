<p><p>The multifunctional high-frequency knives market is segmented into monopolar and bipolar circuits, each serving distinct surgical needs. Monopolar circuit knives utilize a single electrode to deliver high-frequency electrical energy, allowing for precise cutting and coagulation of tissue. In contrast, bipolar circuit knives feature two electrodes, which allow for localized energy application and reduced thermal spread, enhancing safety. Together, these technologies cater to various surgical procedures, improving patient outcomes and operational efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
